Project Ideas

Here is a project that I would love to work on by applying my skills and knowledge and being able to do as much as possible..

Overview

I am very passionate about fitness and living a healthy lifestyle. But watching obesity grow at such an alarming rate around the world makes me feel worried. Being a fitness lover, I feel like taking initiative towards this issue of obesity. I want to make people realize the importance of their health and exercise. So, in order to do so, I wish to create my own fitness app for reaching out to people. I am aware of the fact that there are tons of fitness apps already available in the market but what I want to build is not just a random, easy-going app. I want to add everything that an individual requires in his or her journey of becoming fit because it's not just about gymming, there are other factors to add too.Eating right and feeling good about what you are doing also plays a vital role towards staying fit and healthy. This would not only reduce the risk of several diseases scientifically but also reduce the amount of stress and uncomfortability psychologically that one has about his or her body being obese. My project will be a fitness app that will not only take care of the exercising and diet needs but will also take into account the support and motivation a person needs during the course.

Motivation

According to a survey, 30% of the world's total population is suffering from obesity which is predicted to rise over the course of years. According to an another survey, 58% of smartphone users have downloaded a fitness app. And among these, about 52 percent of users said they downloaded health apps to track physical activity, while 47 percent wanted to track what they ate. Forty-six percent said they downloaded health apps to lose weight and 34 percent said they wanted to use health apps to learn exercises.This clearly shows that people need different apps for different purposes. But I want to combine everything catering to the needs of the people in just one app. Now, with the use of this app, people won't have to switch to separate apps for separate things. This will reduce hassle which, a lot of times, people want to avoid and eventually donot want to commit to the apps. Moreover this will be a free app available to every smartphone user which will grab more amount of customers in the long run as easy feasibility and affordability are the first criteria that needs to be resolved.There aren't much apps that offer everything in free which will be where this project would be different and interesting.

Description

As I already mentioned that I aim to create a whole package in just one app, it will provide the following features: exercising schedule or timetable, diet charts along with the calorie counts of all the included foods, fitness trackers and some motivation needed throughout. Beginning with the exercises, this app will contain different types of exercising plans based on your goals. For example, if a person wants to lose fat, then there will be a separate exercise plan for the fat loss. These different types of plans will contain videos to demonstrate the correct posture for a given exercise.These videos would contain some workout videos of professional bodybuilders. The videos n will be suggesting the sets and repetitions required to do along with amount of calories burnt if followed the plan. In order to keep bringing in some variations within the workout, there will be exercises grouped according to a particular body part to give the customers independency to be able to chose the exercise themselves. Apart from this, each exercise type will elaborate what muscle group it targets along with it's benefits. Moving on to the diet part, this is the main step towards acheiving desired goals. There will be diet plans based again on the goals of an individual along with the calorie counts. The diet plan will undertake every meal including the cheat meals. If a person wants some variations in the diet, then there will be another section of all of the healthy foods. To make this dieting part much easier and at convinience, the meals that will be mentioned in the diet plans will also have recepies of how to prepare them. This will not only save the time of the customer as he or she won't have to look out out for a recipe somewhere else but will also provide them a sense of surity as to what they are cooking is actually what's mentioned. If a person is further interested beyond the natural diet, this app will also provide them a recommendations about health supplements like protein powders, pre-workouts,BCAA's , etc. There will be two different sections of vegetarians and non-vegetarians for the ease of cutomers. Now for the tracking part, the app will be able to track the progress of a person depending on the plan chosen. Other than the gym exercises, it will also be able to track any other physical activity such as running, walking or swimming and give out the calories burnt as a result.It will include the calories required to intake in a day and amount of water needed per day. Coming onto the extras for this app, it will include some motivational quotes as well as motivational videos. These motivational videos could be the stories of some famous people who have acheived what they once desired for. These videos will create a lesser degree of people dropping out of the plan due to lack of motivation. This will help people stay on track until they acheive their goals.

Tools and Technology

For making an app, I would require java in eclipse to code down the app along with java development kit which will create an environment for the language or i could use brackets for HTML5 coding. Eclipse is a platform for writing all the codes of the game and then compiling it so as to run the code. For the app to run, various hardwares have also to be used such as accelerometers, gyroscopes,compases and GPS. A gyroscope is a device that uses Earth’s gravity to help determine the orientation of an object in space; a compass determines cardinal direction, and an accelerometer is designed to measure acceleration.Motion trackers are used to measure walking, running, cycling and swimming activities. With the data collected, smartwatches and apps can calculate speed, cadence, distance traveled, and number of calories burnt.The global positioning system receiver is a valuable feature for runners, cyclists, and hikers.GPS uses 30-some satellites orbiting the Earth to make it possible for users to map their routes, know their exact position, and measure movement speed. These will my requirements for the app.
